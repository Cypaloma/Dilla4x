## Printing Recommendations
- **Material**: PLA (recommended for ease of printing)
- **Layer Height**: 0.2-0.3mm (typical range)
- **Infill**: 15-25% 
- **Supports**: Needed, recommend tree supports touching build plate only
- **Bed Adhesion**: Use brim if needed for warping
